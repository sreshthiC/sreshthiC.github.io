About the Event

আনন্দ সন্ধ্যা

A Joyous Evening

✦

Ananda Sandhya is one of the most cherished annual events of the Bengali Association of Pittsburgh — a joyful evening woven together with music, dance, drama, and the warmth of community.

Join us for an unforgettable celebration of Bengali culture, featuring performances by talented community members spanning all ages. Come for the art, stay for the people.

A beloved tradition since 1981, Ananda Sandhya brings together the Pittsburgh Bengali diaspora every year for an evening of laughter, music, and belonging.
